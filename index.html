Privacy Policy

Official Privacy & Data Legal Terms for ISKCON Hadapsar App

Effective Date: March 6, 2026 Google Play Data Safety Compliant

Hare Krishna! Welcome to the official ISKCON Hadapsar mobile application. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our mobile application, in strict compliance with Google Play Developer policies.

1. Information We Collect

Our app is mostly accessible without creating an account. Most features (like Live Darshan, Kirtan, Daily Schedule) do not require any personal data. We operate on a "No Forced Login" model. However, we process information in the following specific scenarios:

A. Donations & 80G Tax Exemption:

When you voluntarily make a donation through the app, we collect the following information to process the receipt, offer Prasadam, and facilitate 80G tax exemptions:

  • Full Name
  • Mobile Number & Email Address
  • PAN Card details (if you opt for 80G tax exemption)

Note on Financial Data: We do not store your credit/debit card numbers or UPI PINs. All financial transactions are processed securely through regulated, third-party payment gateways.

B. App State & Local Device Storage:

To provide a seamless spiritual experience, we save certain preferences locally on your device storage (Shared Preferences/Local Storage). This data is NOT uploaded to our servers. This includes:

  • Japa Mala counter history and daily rounds.
  • Krishna Leela game levels (Locked/Unlocked status).
  • App themes (Dark/Light mode) and Language selections (Hindi/English).

2. Device Permissions Transparency

To function correctly, our app requests minimal device permissions. We declare the following for complete transparency:

  • Internet & Network State: Required to fetch Live Darshan, Kirtan Media, Youtube videos, and dynamic schedules from our servers.
  • Location (Map Redirection): Our app features a "Temple Directory" and "Map" navigation. We do NOT track, collect, or store your real-time GPS location in the background. Clicking on Map links safely redirects you to third-party mapping applications (like Google Maps).
  • Media & Audio Player: Our app streams Audio/Video (Bhajans, Pravachan). While it controls media playback (Wake Lock to prevent screen sleep during videos), the app NEVER accesses your device microphone or camera. The Japa Mala is a purely touch-based digital counter.

3. External Links, E-commerce & Other Apps

Our app includes buttons and links to our official external E-commerce website where users can purchase physical spiritual items, books, and merchandise. Additionally, we may cross-promote and provide links to download our other official mobile applications and social media channels (YouTube, Facebook, Instagram).

We want to be fully transparent that once you click these links and leave the ISKCON Hadapsar app, your interactions, purchases, and data are governed by the Privacy Policies and Terms of Service of those specific external websites, platforms, or applications.

4. Third-Party Services & Technologies

To ensure the app runs smoothly and efficiently globally, we use trusted third-party services. These services may collect device identifiers and usage data according to their own privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase: Used for app analytics, remote configuration, and real-time database management to ensure the app doesn't crash.
  • Blogger API & YouTube: Used to fetch high-quality images, gallery content, and live video darshan directly to your screen.
  • Advertising (Google AdMob): We may show non-intrusive ads to support the app's server and maintenance costs. Advertising networks may use Advertising IDs (AAID) to serve relevant ads. You can manage personalized ad settings from your phone's Google settings.

5. Push Notifications

We use Firebase Cloud Messaging to send you important spiritual updates, such as Ekadashi reminders, festival alerts, and Live Darshan timings. You can enable or disable these notifications at any time through your device settings.

6. Spiritual Stories, Games & Quizzes (For All Users)

Our app features a dedicated section containing spiritual stories, quizzes, and interactive games (like Memory match and puzzles). These features are designed for users of ALL age groups to engage with Vedic knowledge and improve their spiritual understanding. We want to assure you that no personal data, account logins, or tracking identifiers are collected while using these features. They operate securely without requiring any user information.

7. General Audience Privacy

This application is built for a General Audience and provides spiritual content suitable for everyone. We operate on a strict "No Forced Login" model. We do not knowingly collect, request, or store personal information from any user (including children) just to use the app's primary features, stories, or games. The only time information is explicitly collected is during voluntary monetary donations (which is an action intended for adults). If you are a parent or guardian and believe a minor has submitted personal information through the donation form without your consent, please contact us immediately so we can remove such data.

8. User Feedback & Communication

Our app includes features allowing you to contact our administration via direct WhatsApp integration or Email clients. When you use these features, your phone number or email address becomes visible to our support team. This information is strictly used to address your query and is never shared with or sold to third parties.

9. Data Retention & Deletion Policy

Since our app does not require account creation, there is no user profile to delete. However, we keep your personal data (like donation records) only as long as required for legal, accounting, and tax compliance purposes.

How to Request Data Deletion (Play Store Requirement):

If you wish to delete your past donation records, contact details, or withdraw your consent, you can directly email our administration at iskconhadapsar8@gmail.com with the subject "Data Deletion Request". Upon receiving a verifiable request, we will permanently delete your personal data from our active databases within 15-30 business days.

10. Data Security

We use standard security protocols (including HTTPS/SSL encryption) to protect your data in transit. However, please remember that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.
